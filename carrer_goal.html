My goal:

My goal is to become a business analyst who helps organizations unlock the full potential of their data. I want to be the person everyone turns to when they need answers to key business questions—whether it's understanding what a metric really means, identifying why a marketing channel is underperforming, or pinpointing the reasons behind a sudden drop in leads. I aim to bridge the gap between raw data and strategic decision-making by providing clear, actionable insights that drive business growth. With a deep understanding of both analytics and business strategy, I’m committed to turning complex data into stories that guide companies toward smarter, data-driven decisions.
