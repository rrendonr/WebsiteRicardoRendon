About Me

Originally from Arequipa, Peru, I moved to the U.S. at 19 and began my academic journey at Sierra Community College before transferring to UC Davis. There, I earned my degree in Statistics in 2018, with a minor in Computer Science after discovering my passion for tech. Along the way, I worked at a ski resort in Colorado and traveled to pursue both my studies and personal growth. I’ve had the opportunity to work at three amazing companies—COOP, where I helped undergrads land their first data analytics roles; Allstate, as a Data Analyst; and most recently at Lennar, where I gained valuable experience applying data insights to drive business decisions. Throughout these experiences, I’ve honed my ability to leverage data to solve complex business problems and provide actionable insights. I’m now eager to take the next step in my career, using my skills and passion for data analysis to help organizations make data-driven decisions and fuel business growth.
